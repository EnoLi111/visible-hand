THE INVISIBLE WORKFORCE

Equality is not equally accessible.

Women are disproportionately affected by precarious employment including casual labour, insecure contracts, and unpaid care responsibilities.


Existing workplace equality systems often fail to reach workers outside formal employment structures, creating invisible forms of labour inequality.
