Workplace inequality still shapes the lives of many women around the world.

Building safer
futures for women
in precarious work.

This project explores how gender inequality, emotional labour, and unstable employment conditions impact women workers. Through research and visual storytelling, we aim to create awareness and inspire structural change.

Women at Work

Our Process

1

Research

We explored feminist theory, academic journals, policy reports and case studies surrounding precarious labour and gender inequality.

2

Analysis

We analysed the emotional and social effects of unstable employment on women workers, especially within patriarchal systems.

3

Intervention

We transformed our research into an interactive digital intervention designed to communicate workplace inequality through design and storytelling.
